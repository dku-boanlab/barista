   8 /****************************************************************
   9  *
  10  * High Level Design Notes
  11  *
  12  * Hardware tables are managed field_control_t and entry_control_t
  13  * structures in hw_flow.h; these roughly mirror the HW structure
  14  * and are preallocated.
  15  *
  16  * The glue between these and SW flows are provided by the HW flow
  17  * objects (also in hw_flow.h) which are dynamically allocated and
  18  * logically extend the SW flow object.
  19  *
  20  * A linear table of HW flow objects is maintained, mostly drawn
  21  * from the existing table-linear.c implementation in udatapath.
  22  *
  23  */
